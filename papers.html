SPARTA Papers

The following paper is the best reference for citing SPARTA. It describes the DSMC and parallel algorithms used in the code. Please also give the URL of the SPARTA web site in your paper, namely http://sparta.github.io.

Direct Simulation Monte Carlo on petaflop supercomputers and beyond, S. J. Plimpton, S. G. Moore, A. Borner, A. K. Stagg, T. P. Koehler, J. R. Torczynski, M. A. Gallis, Physics of Fluids, 31, 086101 (2019). (abstract)
